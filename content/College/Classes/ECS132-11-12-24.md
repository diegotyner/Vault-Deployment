  - **Variance/Standard Deviation:** Measures of data spread or dispersion.
- **Key Formulas:**
  - Confidence Interval:  $\bar{x} \pm z \frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}}$ 
  - Variance of Sample: $\frac{s^2}{n}$ 
- **Critical Nuances:**
  - Difference between sample and population variance.
